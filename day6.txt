COMMUNICATION PRACTICE

Speak for 3 minutes on:

Explain one challenge you faced while learning coding

Include:
- What was the challenge?
- Why was it difficult?
- How did you overcome it?
- What did you learn from it?


When I started learning to code, my biggest problem was logic.
I would read a question, but it took me a long time to understand how to solve it.
It was difficult because I tried to solve everything directly on the screen. 
I would look at the blank editor and feel stuck. 
I solved this problem by stepping away from the computer. I started using a pen and paper.
Now, I always use a pen and paper first. It saves me a lot of time and prevents many mistakes.
