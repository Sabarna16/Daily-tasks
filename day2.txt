Question 1
A shopkeeper buys a book for ₹400 and sells it for ₹480.
Find the profit percentage.

Solution:
Give: cost price = 400,sellig price = 480
profit = sp-cp
       = 480-40
       = 80
profit percentage = sp-cp/cp*100
                  = 80/400*100
             Ans  = 20%
