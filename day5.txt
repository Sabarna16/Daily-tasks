Question 3
Two trains moving in opposite directions at 50 km/h and 70 km/h.
Find relative speed.

Solution:
trains moving opposite directions at 50km/h and 70km/h
50+70 = 120km/h

Question 4
A person walks at 5 km/h and runs at 10 km/h.
Find time taken to cover 15 km if half distance is walked and half is run.

Solution:
total distance = 15km
half distance = 7.5km
walking time = 7.5/5=1.5hour
running time = 7.5/10=0.75hours
time = 1.5+0.75=2.25hours

Question 5
If a train crosses a pole in 10 seconds and its length is 100 meters,
find its speed.

Solution:
speed = distance/time
      = 100/10 = 10m/s
