APTITUDE (Ratio & Proportion)

Question 1
If the ratio of A:B is 3:5 and B:C is 2:3, find A:C.

Solution:
A:B=3:5,B:C=2:3,A:C=?
A/C=A/B*B/C
  3*2=6
  5*3=15
A/C=6/15=2:5

Question 2
Divide ₹1000 in the ratio 2:3:5.

Solution:

=1000/10=100
