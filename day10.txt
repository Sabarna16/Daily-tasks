

APTITUDE (Speed, Time & Distance)

Question 1
A car travels 60 km in 1 hour. What is the speed?
Solution:
Distance = 60km,time = 1 hour,speed = ?
speed = distance/time=60/1=60km/hr

Question 2
A train travels at 50 km/hr. How long will it take to travel 150 km?
Solution:
Distance = 150km,speed = 50km/hr
time = distance/speed=150/50=3hrs


Question 3
If a person walks at 5 km/hr, how far will they walk in 3 hours?
Solution:
speed = 5km/hr
time = 3hrs
distance = speed*time = 5*3=15km

Question 4
Two trains move in opposite direction at 40 km/hr and 60 km/hr.
Find relative speed.
Solution:
relative speed = v1+v2=40+60=100km/hr


Question 5
A car travels first half distance at 40 km/hr and second half at 60 km/hr.
Find average speed.
Solution:
Average speed = 2v1v2/v+v2= 2*40*60/40+60=4800/100=48km/hr

The most important rule for Binary Search is that it only works on a sorted array. Whether it’s numbers in ascending 
order or words in alphabetical order, the data must be organized.
the algorithm looks at the middle element of the entire array.
It compares that middle element to the target value you are looking for.
It keeps dividing the search space until the item is found or the list runs out.
Time Complexity is O(log n).
